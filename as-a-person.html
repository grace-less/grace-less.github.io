As a Person

I can think of myself as many different things but one that rings true and probably fits me best is family oriented. I’m not talking about the family I was born with. I’m talking about the family I chose. There are a few I was born with in my life that I want to know little else about them besides that they’re still breathing somewhere on this planet. However, there are lots of people in my life and many of them, I have chosen to keep there. I’ve adopted parents, siblings, and cousins into my chosen family. They have taken care of me in times of need, just as I have done anything I can to take care of them in return. -SGD
