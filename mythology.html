Mythology

Around the world, there are whispers of stories about impossible things. They’re then shared in more whispers, growing and becoming more outlandish. As time passes and the stories are told over and over, becoming stranger and stranger, they become myths and legends. No matter where you go, you can find one about something that may seem unusual. Perhaps you might find one that sounds familiar but goes by a different name. These wild claims have survived for centuries, but if there’s no truth to them, then why do we continue to tell their stories? -SGD
